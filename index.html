Cris Hernández is a typeface designer and researcher with a practice centered on supporting the Indigenous languages and writing systems of North America.

Que Queda Type (Spanish for 'what remains') is a new foundry currently in development, with typefaces that reflect this commitment and a long-term goal of expanding its reach to Central and South America.

Working from Brooklyn; Lenapehoking, the unceded homeland of the Lenape people.
